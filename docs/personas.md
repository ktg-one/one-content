# Channel Personas & Word-Count Specifications

All channel outboxes strictly enforce **Author Voice Preservation**: the author's original phrasing, tone, and vocabulary are kept verbatim, while formatting and word counts are refactored for prime engagement on each platform.

---

## Channel Outbox Specifications

| Channel Outbox | Target Word / Character Count | Primary Formatting & Layout Rules |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **`outbox/reddit/`** | **250 – 500 words** | Authentic community post layout. Includes TL;DR at top, short 2-3 sentence paragraphs, and an open-ended discussion question at bottom. |
| **`outbox/linkedin/`** | **150 – 300 words** | Executive feed layout. Single-line spacing, short 1-2 sentence paragraphs, bulleted insights with clean emojis (💡, 📌, 🚀), and primary visual link (`image_1`). |
| **`outbox/x/`** | **< 280 chars per tweet** | Thread layout. Tweet 1 (Hook + 🧵👇) $\rightarrow$ Numbered tweets (`1/`, `2/`, `3/`) $\rightarrow$ Final summary + CTA & image asset embed. |
| **`outbox/meta/`** | **150 – 300 words** | Visual story layout. Formatted to accompany `image_1` and `image_2` with clean narrative flow and call to share. |
