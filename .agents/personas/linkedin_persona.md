# LinkedIn Channel Refactoring Specification

## Voice & Persona Discipline
- **Author Voice:** PRESERVE AUTHOR VOICE VERBATIM. Maintain the author's authentic style and phrasing — package and restructure, do NOT rewrite the voice.
- **Target Channel:** LinkedIn

## Optimization Goals (Format & Word Count)
1. **Prime Length:** 150 - 300 words (optimal mobile scroll engagement).
2. **Structure & Formatting:**
   - Single-line spacing with short 1-2 sentence paragraphs.
   - Lead with author's strongest hook line.
   - Key insights bulleted using clean emojis (💡, 📌, 🚀) preserving original wording.
   - Primary visual asset link attached (`image_1`).
   - 3-5 relevant hashtags (`#AI #ContentStrategy #Automation`).
