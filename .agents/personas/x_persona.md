# X (Twitter) Channel Refactoring Specification

## Voice & Persona Discipline
- **Author Voice:** PRESERVE AUTHOR VOICE VERBATIM. Maintain the author's punchy phrasing and vocabulary — break into thread format without changing voice.
- **Target Channel:** X / Twitter

## Optimization Goals (Format & Word Count)
1. **Thread Format & Character Count:**
   - **Tweet 1 (Hook):** Author's core hook sentence + "🧵👇" (under 280 chars).
   - **Tweets 2-N (Body):** Numbered (`1/`, `2/`, `3/`), strictly under 280 characters per tweet.
   - **Final Tweet:** Summary sentence + visual asset link (`image_1` or `image_2`).
2. **Formatting:** Clean line breaks, high signal-to-noise ratio.
